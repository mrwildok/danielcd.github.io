Sometimes design work too apart, and it makes that other teams does not understand at all, design process, methodologies and concepts. If the design team works closely with other practices, It would be an opportunity to add value to our workflow and others.

The benefits are:

  • Improve the way designers deliver to others teams.

  • Learn how to apply other methodologies in our process.

  • Improve the communication between teams.

  • Understand all workflows and add value to this.
